Child Safety Standards

LokaLinc is committed to maintaining a safe environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy for any content or behavior related to child sexual abuse or exploitation.

Our Commitment

  • We strictly prohibit any content involving child sexual abuse or exploitation.
  • Users can report inappropriate content or behavior at any time.
  • We take immediate action to remove violating content and suspend or ban users involved.

Reporting

If you encounter any content or behavior that may violate these standards, please report it immediately:

  • Email: support@lokalinc.de

Compliance

LokaLinc complies with all applicable child safety laws and works with relevant authorities where required.
